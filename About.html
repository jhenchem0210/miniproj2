About Us

A tale of two different individuals who shares the same vision on travelling and has the passion to explore the beauty of our country Philippines. With the same goal to promote and even more love the country. Go Pilipinas Adventurista was then established with the help of local government in the tourism campaigns such as "Love Philippines", "Go Local" and "goPilipinas". ... Inline with this, Our long-term goals are to produce as much high-quality content as we can, covering as many locations as we can. All of which will facilitate travel and, ideally, give users of Google the information they seek. When creating material, we make an effort to create in-depth blogs, use attractive photographs, recommend amazing locations and activities, and strive to make the posts simple to read. We believe that "Anyone can travel!"

Founders In Action


We travel, some of us forever, to seek other places, other lives, other souls. To develop, learn, and grow, you must go out into the world. If you have never seen other cultures or viewpoints from completely different people, how can you truly know who you are and what you believe in? This site will help you decide on the perfect place for your next journey. And love our own country’s finest tourism.

We are inspired to see, taste, and try new things when we travel because it forces us out of our comfort zones. It continuously tests our ability not only to adapt to and explore new environments, but also to interact with other people, welcome new experiences as they arrive, and share them with friends and loved ones.
